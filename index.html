Brighton Nightlife

There are countless numbers of clubs, pubs and bars in Brighton; with so much choice it can be hard to know which ones are worth visiting. This helpful guide will breakdown five of the most popular choices in Brighton to see which one would suit you best, from after-work evening drinks to all out clubbing.

Revolution

One of the best known party bars in Brighton, no night at Revolution is a quiet one. From fun-filled DJ nights to cracking drink deals, this stylish bar in Brighton town centre is for the young party people of the city. Revolution is only a 10 minute walk from Brighton Station, so you can start your night out in no time with happy hour cocktails from 5pm. They also host regular DJ nights every weekend, with drink deals and a brilliant party atmosphere that will see you partying until the early hours. If it becomes too raucous through the weekend, there is always the Courtyard for a more chilled-out party vibe.

Patterns

Patterns is a favourite fronting an eclectic DJ roster and forward-thinking party antics, where legendary artists such as Daft Punk, The Stone Roses, The Chemical Brothers, Carl Cox and Paul Oakenfold have all graced the premises. Located in the former home of the iconic Audio club, Patterns reopened on the 13th May 2015 unveiling a new name and redesign, giving the iconic seafront and its musical mecca a new lease of life. You sshould expect a vibrant space for creative crowds with a thirst for quality street food and drink with world-class talent from the live and electronic underground. The south-facing sea view terrace also provides a great spot for lazy summer afternoons until it’s time to hit the bar.

No 32

A party palace of drinking, dining and clubbing extravagance, No 32 is located in The Lanes and offers up an incredible atmosphere for your nights out in Brighton. Filling up hungry punters with their spread of fashionable burgers, traditional grills and snacking platters alongside your drinks, it’s a great place to stop in if you’re feeling famished. You'll also find a bar well versed in the art of mixology with a strong show on the cocktail front; serving a balance of the classics and modern favourites alongside a notable choice of craft beers. Get 2 for 1 on certain mixed drinks every weekday between 5pm and 7pm.

Walkabout

Alongside serving all your favourite American comfort food, the Walkabout on Brighton's Beachfront is fast becoming one of the city's most popular clubs. The foods will follow suit with the country's other spread of Walkabout watering holes where a pub grub menu is the name of the game and sharing platters are the perfect grazing boards for all you sporting fiends out there. If that wasn't enough, you know you can count on Walkabout to bring the booze hard: beer, wine and cocktails galore gallivant here. You'll be able to sit back in booths with mates, hire out a private space and experience live music or club nights at this fantastic venue. Look no further for your new favourite nightclub in Brighton.

Pryzm

Arguably Brighton's busiest and certainly biggest club, Pryzm has separate themed rooms including an 80s throwback section complete with flashing dance floor, the urban Curve and the main room. With its three club arenas and a luxurious VIP suite, Pryzm offers a truly elegant bar experience in Brighton like no other. Expect bespoke designer furnishings, world class entertainment and premium service standards, in this wonderfully atmospheric party space with a whole range of VIP areas and booths.
